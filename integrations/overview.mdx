## Integration Categories

### Email & Calendar

Connect your email and calendar to Twenty to:
- Automatically sync emails with related contacts and companies
- View email threads directly in Twenty
- Sync calendar events and schedule meetings
- Send emails from within Twenty

Supported providers:
- **Google Workspace** - Gmail and Google Calendar
- **Microsoft 365** - Outlook and Microsoft Calendar

### Automation & Workflows

Automate your workflows and connect Twenty with thousands of apps:
- **Zapier** - Build automated workflows without code
- **Webhooks** - Trigger custom actions on record changes
- **API** - Build custom integrations using GraphQL

### Communication

Stay connected with your team:
- **Slack** - Receive notifications and updates in Slack channels
- **Webhook notifications** - Send custom notifications to any service

## How to Enable Integrations

Most integrations can be enabled from within your Twenty workspace:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Click on your workspace name in the top-left corner, then select **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    In the settings menu, navigate to the **Integrations** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Account">
    Find the integration you want to enable and click **Connect**. You'll be redirected to authenticate with the service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Once connected, configure any additional settings like sync frequency or notification preferences.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Some integrations may require administrator permissions in your workspace. Contact your workspace admin if you don't see the option to enable an integration.
</Note>
