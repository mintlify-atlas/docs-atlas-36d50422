## Accessing billing settings

To manage your billing:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open settings">
    Click on your workspace name in the bottom left, then select **Settings**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to billing">
    In the settings sidebar, click on **Billing**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View your plan">
    Your current plan and usage information are displayed at the top of the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upgrading your plan

To upgrade to a higher plan:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to billing settings">
    Navigate to Settings → Billing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    Click **Upgrade** on the plan you want to switch to.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter payment information">
    Provide your billing details and payment method.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm upgrade">
    Review the changes and confirm your upgrade. Your new plan takes effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  When you upgrade, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remaining time in your billing period.
</Info>

## Usage monitoring

Twenty tracks usage of various features to help you understand your consumption:

- **Workflow executions**: Number of workflow runs per month
- **API requests**: GraphQL and REST API call volume
- **Storage**: Total file storage used
- **Records**: Number of records across all objects

View your current usage in the Billing settings page.

## Payment methods

Twenty accepts the following payment methods:

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- ACH transfers (for annual Enterprise plans)

<Warning>
  Keep your payment method up to date to avoid service interruptions.
</Warning>

## Billing cycle

- **Monthly plans**: Billed on the same day each month
- **Annual plans**: Billed once per year with a discount

You can change your billing cycle at any time from the billing settings.

## Invoices

All invoices are automatically generated and sent to your billing email address. You can also download invoices from the Billing settings page.

To access invoice history:

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Scroll to the **Invoice history** section
3. Click on any invoice to download the PDF

## Cancellation

You can cancel your Twenty subscription at any time:

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click **Cancel subscription**
3. Confirm your cancellation

<Warning>
  When you cancel, your workspace will continue to function until the end of your current billing period, after which it will be downgraded or archived.
</Warning>

Your data remains accessible even after cancellation. You can export all data before the end of your billing period.

## Self-hosted billing

For self-hosted deployments:

- **Community edition**: Free forever with core features
- **Enterprise license**: Required for enterprise features like SSO, advanced security, and dedicated support

Contact Twenty sales for enterprise licensing options for self-hosted deployments.
