Activities in Twenty provide a complete history of all interactions with your contacts and companies. The timeline shows emails, calls, meetings, notes, and tasks in chronological order.

## What are activities?

Activities are records of interactions and events related to your CRM records. They help you:

- Track communication history
- See what happened and when
- Plan follow-up actions
- Collaborate with your team

### Activity types

Twenty tracks several types of activities:

- **Emails**: Synced from your connected email accounts
- **Calendar events**: Meetings and calls from your calendar
- **Notes**: Manual notes you add about interactions
- **Tasks**: To-do items related to records
- **Comments**: Team discussions on records

## Viewing the timeline

The activity timeline appears on every record's detail page:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a record">
    Click on any company, person, or opportunity to open its detail page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View the timeline">
    The timeline is displayed on the right side of the page, showing activities in reverse chronological order (newest first).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filter activities">
    Use the filter buttons above the timeline to show only specific activity types.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding activities

You can manually add activities to any record:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Adding a note">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click add note">
        In the timeline, click the **+ Note** button.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Write your note">
        Type your note content. You can use rich text formatting:
        - **Bold** and *italic* text
        - Bullet lists
        - @mentions to notify team members
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Save the note">
        Click **Save** or press Cmd/Ctrl + Enter.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Adding a task">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click add task">
        Click the **+ Task** button in the timeline.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Enter task details">
        Provide:
        - Task title
        - Due date
        - Assigned to (team member)
        - Priority (low, medium, high)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Create task">
        Click **Create** to add the task to the timeline.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Logging a call">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click log activity">
        Click the **+ Activity** button and select **Call**.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Add call details">
        Enter:
        - Call duration
        - Call outcome
        - Notes from the call
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Save activity">
        Click **Save** to add the call to the timeline.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Email integration

When you connect your email account, Twenty automatically:

- Syncs emails to/from contacts in your CRM
- Displays emails in the timeline
- Links emails to the relevant records

<Info>
  See [Email Integration](/user-guide/calendar-emails/email-integration) for setup instructions.
</Info>

### Email in timeline

Emails appear in the timeline with:
- Subject line
- Sender and recipients
- Email preview
- Timestamp

Click on any email to view the full content.

## Calendar events

Meetings and calls from your synced calendar automatically appear in the timeline:

- Past events show as completed activities
- Upcoming events show with a countdown
- Event details include attendees, time, and location

<Tip>
  Connect your calendar to automatically track all customer meetings.
</Tip>

## Tasks and reminders

Tasks help you track follow-up actions:

### Task properties

- **Title**: What needs to be done
- **Due date**: When it should be completed
- **Assigned to**: Who is responsible
- **Priority**: Low, medium, or high
- **Status**: To do, in progress, done

### Task notifications

You receive notifications for:
- Tasks assigned to you
- Tasks due today or overdue
- Tasks completed by others

## Filtering the timeline

Narrow down what you see in the timeline:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="By activity type">
    Click the filter buttons above the timeline:
    - **All**: Show everything
    - **Emails**: Only emails
    - **Meetings**: Only calendar events
    - **Notes**: Only notes
    - **Tasks**: Only tasks
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="By date range">
    Use the date picker to show activities from:
    - Last 7 days
    - Last 30 days
    - This quarter
    - All time
    - Custom range
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="By team member">
    Filter to show only activities:
    - Created by specific users
    - Assigned to specific users
    - Involving specific users
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Comments and collaboration

Comments let your team discuss records:

1. Click **Add comment** in the timeline
2. Type your message
3. Use @mentions to notify specific team members
4. Click **Post** to add the comment

Comments appear in the timeline and team members receive notifications for @mentions.

## Activity notifications

Stay informed about activities:

- **In-app notifications**: Red badge on the bell icon
- **Email digests**: Daily or weekly summaries
- **Browser notifications**: Real-time alerts (if enabled)

Configure notification preferences in Settings → Notifications.

## Bulk operations

Manage multiple activities at once:

- **Mark tasks complete**: Select multiple tasks and mark as done
- **Reassign tasks**: Change the assignee for several tasks
- **Delete activities**: Remove obsolete entries
