Account settings let you customize your personal Twenty experience, manage your profile, and configure security options.

## Accessing account settings

To access your account settings:

1. Click on your avatar in the bottom left corner
2. Select **Settings** from the menu
3. Click on **Account** in the settings sidebar

## Profile information

Update your personal information:

### Basic details

- **Name**: Your display name shown to other users
- **Email**: Your email address (used for login and notifications)
- **Avatar**: Profile picture displayed throughout Twenty
- **Job title**: Your role in the organization
- **Phone**: Your contact phone number

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click edit profile">
    In the Account settings, click **Edit Profile**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update your information">
    Make changes to any of the fields.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload avatar">
    Click on your avatar to upload a new profile picture (JPG, PNG, or GIF up to 5MB).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save** to apply your updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Security settings

Protect your account with these security features:

### Password

Change your password regularly:

1. Go to Account → Security
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **Update Password**

<Warning>
  Choose a strong password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
</Warning>

### Two-factor authentication

Add an extra layer of security:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    In Security settings, click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose method">
    Select your preferred 2FA method:
    - **Authenticator app** (recommended): Use Google Authenticator, Authy, or similar
    - **SMS**: Receive codes via text message
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set up authentication">
    Follow the setup wizard:
    - For authenticator apps: Scan the QR code
    - For SMS: Verify your phone number
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save recovery codes">
    Store the recovery codes in a safe place. You'll need these if you lose access to your 2FA device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Authenticator apps are more secure than SMS because they work offline and are harder to intercept.
</Tip>

### Active sessions

View and manage your active login sessions:

- See all devices where you're logged in
- View session location and browser
- Revoke access from specific devices

To sign out of all sessions except your current one, click **Sign out everywhere**.

## Notification preferences

Customize how and when you receive notifications:

### Notification channels

- **In-app**: Notifications within Twenty (bell icon)
- **Email**: Notification emails sent to your inbox
- **Browser**: Push notifications in your web browser

### Notification types

Configure notifications for:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Activity">
    - Tasks assigned to you
    - Comments on your records
    - @mentions in notes
    - Reminders for upcoming tasks
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Collaboration">
    - Team members edit your records
    - Someone shares a view with you
    - Workflow executions affecting your records
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Updates">
    - New workspace features
    - System maintenance notifications
    - Product updates
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Email digest

Receive a summary of notifications:

- **Frequency**: Daily, weekly, or never
- **Time**: Choose what time to receive the digest
- **Content**: Select which notification types to include

## Language and region

Set your localization preferences:

- **Language**: Choose your preferred language for the interface
- **Timezone**: Set your timezone for accurate timestamps
- **Date format**: Select how dates are displayed (MM/DD/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY, etc.)
- **Time format**: 12-hour or 24-hour time
- **First day of week**: Sunday or Monday

## Appearance

Customize the look of Twenty:

- **Theme**: Light, dark, or system default
- **Density**: Comfortable, compact, or spacious
- **Font size**: Small, medium, or large

<Info>
  Appearance settings are saved per device, so you can use different themes on different computers.
</Info>

## Keyboard shortcuts

View and customize keyboard shortcuts:

1. Go to Account → Shortcuts
2. See the list of available shortcuts
3. Click on any shortcut to customize it
4. Press **Reset to defaults** to restore original shortcuts

Common shortcuts:
- `Cmd/Ctrl + K`: Open command menu
- `Cmd/Ctrl + /`: Show keyboard shortcuts
- `N`: Create new record
- `G` then `H`: Go home

## API tokens

Generate personal API tokens for integrations:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to API tokens">
    Go to Account → API Tokens.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create new token">
    Click **Generate New Token**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Name your token">
    Give it a descriptive name (e.g., "My integration script").
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set permissions">
    Choose what the token can access (read-only or read-write).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Copy token">
    Copy the token immediately - you won't be able to see it again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Treat API tokens like passwords. Don't share them or commit them to version control.
</Warning>

## Delete account

If you need to delete your Twenty account:

<Warning>
  Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to account settings">
    Navigate to Account → Danger Zone.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click delete account">
    Click the **Delete Account** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Type your password to confirm.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Final confirmation">
    Type "DELETE" to proceed with account deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Before deleting your account, consider:
- Exporting your data
- Transferring record ownership to another user
- Leaving workspaces you're a member of
